R&W Words in Context
4-Step Strategy
1. CoverBlank out the word mentally
2. ContextRead sentence + surrounding lines
3. PredictForm your own word before reading choices
4. MatchPick the choice closest to your prediction
⚠️ Most common definition = most common WRONG answer. They always use the word in a more specific way.

vener-venerate = deeply respect

R&W Hard Comprehension Questions
TypeKey Rule
Command of Evidence (text)Quote must DIRECTLY prove the claim. Related ≠ right.
Command of Evidence (data)Find the exact data point. Close ≠ correct.
InferenceMust be true if text is true. No outside knowledge. Avoid extremes (always/never).
Text Structure/PurposeDescribe the FUNCTION (why), not the content (what).
Cross-textSummarize each passage → identify agreement/disagreement → text only.
Hard Q strategy: go back to passage FIRST, form your own answer, THEN read choices.
