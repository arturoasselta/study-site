Vectors & Projectile Motion (Unit 3)
FormulaNotes
Vₓ = V·cosθHorizontal component
Vᵧ = V·sinθVertical component
V = √(Vₓ²+Vᵧ²)Resultant magnitude
θ = tan⁻¹(Vᵧ/Vₓ)Direction angle
Projectile Rules
  • Horizontal: constant velocity, a=0 → x = Vₓt
  • Vertical: free fall → use kinematics with a=−9.8 m/s²
  • At peak: Vᵧ = 0
  • Horizontal & vertical are independent
