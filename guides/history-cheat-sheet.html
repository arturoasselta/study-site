Essential Vocabulary
  • Popular Sovereignty — people vote on slavery in territories
  • Nullification — state's right to void federal law (Calhoun)
  • Sectionalism — regional interests over national unity
  • Imperialism — extending power/control over other nations
  • Laissez-faire — minimal government economic intervention
  • Isolationism — avoid foreign entanglements
  • Détente — Cold War relaxation of tensions (Nixon)
  • Carpetbagger — Northern Republican who moved South post-Civil War
  • Jim Crow Laws — state laws enforcing racial segregation
